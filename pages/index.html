Resonara

Offline long-form text-to-speech

Resonara turns documents into chaptered speech on your machine — multi-engine neural TTS (Piper, Kokoro, optional expressive), English + pt-BR, optional Whisper quality gates.

Not an audio lab. Not a piano. Not a podcast host. Text → voice, offline.

  • English + pt-BR neural voices
  • Document import → chaptered audio
  • Library, CLI, watch folder
  • Diagnostics stay local — no telemetry
