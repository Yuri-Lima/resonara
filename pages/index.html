Resonara 2.0

Shape sound. Speak the long form. Play freely.

Resonara is an offline desktop audio lab: sample piano, FFmpeg tools, and neural long-form TTS with Piper and Kokoro — plus Whisper QA, an audiobook library, podcast feeds, and full Portuguese (Brasil) support.

Not just platform voices. Not cloud-bound. Your models, your machine.

  • Library-first bookshelf with covers and resume
  • CLI, watch folder, EPUB media overlays
  • Diagnostics stay local — no telemetry
