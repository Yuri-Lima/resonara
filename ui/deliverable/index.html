    Prosody engineering — pauses that breathe
    
      
    
        Seam-elimination (trim + 20 ms crossfade) fixed clicks by erasing
        sentence and paragraph silence. The pause architecture restores
        professional audiobook rhythm: boundary-typed gaps, three profiles,
        a measured probe contract, and regression pins so the next audio fix
        cannot silently kill pauses again.
