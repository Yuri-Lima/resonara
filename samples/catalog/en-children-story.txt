# The Star That Learned to Wait

Once there was a little star that wanted to shine brighter than all the others. It rushed every twinkle and forgot to breathe between them.

"Why do you hurry so?" asked the Moon in a calm voice.

"Because the sky is large and I am afraid of falling behind," said the star.

The Moon smiled. "The stars that time loves most are the ones that know how to pause. When you rest, the people below have time to find you."

And so the star learned: shining well also means knowing when to be still.
