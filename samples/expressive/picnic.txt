The meadow grew bright as the first laugh left her lips. She did not hesitate. She simply lit up, as if the joy of the day had become too sweet to keep another moment.

He held her hand until the sunlight danced. There was everything left to say. The clock on the blanket kept time for a world that fully included her.

Outside, bees traced quick paths through the flowers. Inside the basket, happiness settled into every corner like honey that would never run dry.
