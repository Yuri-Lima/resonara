Once upon a time, a tiny fox named Pip found a glowing acorn under the oldest oak in the forest. "Oh my stars!" squeaked Pip. "What a treasure!"

The acorn whispered secrets of the moon, and Pip's whiskers tingled with wonder. Away he dashed — hop, skip, tumble — to show his friends.

And from that night on, whenever the moon was full, the forest danced with golden light, and little Pip laughed the brightest of all.
