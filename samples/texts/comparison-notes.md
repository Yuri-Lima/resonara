# Platform vs Piper listening notes

- **Platform (macOS say):** clear but flat; limited prosody; fine for short UI prompts.
- **Piper (en_US-lessac-medium):** warmer neural prosody; natural sentence flow within chunks.
- **Seams:** with trim + 20ms crossfade, paragraph demos show no audible clicks.
- **RTF (this machine):** quick-sentence ~2.8×, paragraph ~8× real-time with Piper Python wheel.
