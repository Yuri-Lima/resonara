O novo framework da startup de São Paulo utiliza machine learning para otimizar a user experience. Segundo o CTO, a equipe local prefere documentar tudo em português claro.

Our approach leverages state-of-the-art NLP models, explained the visiting engineer during the meetup in English.

In the afternoon workshop, participants discussed latency budgets and offline-first caching strategies for mobile apps.
