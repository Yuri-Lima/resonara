# Chapter One: The Approach

The trail narrowed as the forest thickened. Birds fell silent near the ridge. A cold wind came down from the north.

## Section A — First Light

Morning mist clung to the ferns. A single boot print marked the mud beside the path.

### Topic: Water Sources

Find the spring east of the fallen oak. Fill bottles before noon. Do not drink from the stagnant pool.
